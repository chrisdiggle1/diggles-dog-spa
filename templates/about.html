About Diggles Dog Spa

Welcome to Diggles Dog Spa! your premier destination for all things dog care. We offer a variety of services to keep your furry friends happy and healthy. From grooming to daycare, our team is dedicated to providing top-notch care in a safe and welcoming environment. Nestled in the heart of our community, we pride ourselves on being more than just a service provider; we're a home away from home for your beloved pets. Our mission is to deliver unparalleled grooming and wellness services in a safe, nurturing, and eco-friendly environment.

Our Story

Founded on a deep passion for animals, Diggles Dog Spa started as a small grooming service by a team of dog lovers who wanted to make a difference in pet care. With years of experience and a shared vision of providing exceptional care, we have grown into a full-service dog spa that caters to every aspect of your dog's well-being.

Our Services

  • Full-Service Grooming: Includes a bath with natural shampoo, blow dry, full-body haircut, ear cleaning, nail trimming, and anal gland expression.
  • Basic Bath & Tidy: For dogs that need a simple clean-up. Includes a bath, blow dry, light trimming (if needed), ear cleaning, and nail trim.
  • Puppy Introduction to Grooming: A gentle introduction to grooming for puppies up to 6 months old, including a bath, light brushing, and nail trim.
  • De-Shedding Treatment: A special treatment to reduce shedding, including a bath with a de-shedding shampoo, blow dry, and thorough brushing to remove loose fur.
  • Spa Packages: Luxurious treatments including aromatherapy baths, moisturizing paw and nose treatments, and relaxing massages.
  • Teeth Brushing: Dental hygiene session including teeth brushing and breath freshening.
  • Nail Trimming & Grinding: Nail trimming to a safe length and grinding to smooth out rough edges.
  • Ear Cleaning: Gentle cleaning of the ears to remove dirt and wax, helping to prevent infections.
  • Eye Cleaning: Careful cleaning around the eyes to remove tear stains.
  • Flea & Tick Treatment: A deep-cleansing bath with flea and tick treatment to rid your pet of pests. (Note: This should not replace regular flea and tick prevention methods.)
