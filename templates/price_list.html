Early grooming is not just beneficial for puppies; it's crucial for their development and well-being. Starting at around 10 to 12 weeks of age, shortly after their second vaccination, introduces them to grooming in a positive and stress-free manner. At Diggles Dog Spa, we specialize in making your puppy's first grooming experience enjoyable and full of gentle care. Our expert team focuses on building trust and comfort, ensuring that your puppy views grooming as a time for pampering. By introducing them to professional grooming early on, we help instill good grooming habits, setting the stage for a lifetime of healthy grooming routines. Choose Diggles Dog Spa for a nurturing introduction to grooming, tailored to meet the unique needs of your growing puppy.
